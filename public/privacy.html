Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This privacy policy explains how 27 Pictures (we, us) handles personal data collected through twentyseven.pictures.

What We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily submit through the contact form on our website: your name and email address. We do not collect any other personal data.

How We Use It

Your name and email are used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not use them for marketing, we do not sell them, and we do not share them with third parties except as necessary to deliver our reply (via our email service provider).

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or analytics tools. Our website does not track your behaviour, device, or browsing history.

Our contact form uses Cloudflare Turnstile for spam protection. Turnstile may set a technical cookie. You can read Cloudflare's privacy policy at cloudflare.com/privacypolicy.

Data Retention

Inquiry emails are retained for up to 12 months and then deleted. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, email us at info@twentyseven.pictures.
